Microblading eyebrows

(AI) Microblading is a semi-permanent cosmetic procedure that involves using a specialized tool to implant pigment into the skin to create natural-looking, fuller eyebrows. The process is often used to enhance the appearance of eyebrows by filling in sparse areas, reshaping the brows, or creating a more defined arch. Microblading is typically performed by a trained and certified microblading technician, who uses a hand-held tool with ultra-fine needles to create hair-like strokes that mimic the natural appearance of eyebrow hairs.

How long microblading last?(AI after) The results of microblading can last anywhere from 1 to 3 years, but can vary from person to person, as it depends on factors such as skin type, lifestyle, and aftercare.

What is Micronnedling

Microneedling, also known as collagen induction therapy, is a cosmetic procedure that involves the use of a device with fine needles to create tiny punctures in the skin. These micro-injuries stimulate the body's natural wound healing process, which results in increased collagen and elastin production. This helps to improve the texture and firmness of the skin, reduce the appearance of scars, fine lines, and wrinkles, and promote overall skin rejuvenation.
