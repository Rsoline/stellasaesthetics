Skin Booster Prophilo

Skin Booster Prophilo is a type of injectable treatment that combines hyaluronic acid and a high concentration of bio-remodeling agents. Not only design to improve skin firmness, hydration and elasticity, but it remodels ageing and sagging skin by stimulating the production of 4 different types of collagen and elastin. Prophilo can help with skin laxity, fine lines, and loss of volume in areas such as the face, neck, and hands.

How often prophilo is injected?

Prophilo is typically injected in two treatment sessions, spaced one month apart. After the initial two treatments, maintenance treatments are usually recommended every 6 months to maintain the results. However, the exact frequency of injections may vary depending on individual needs and the recommendation of your healthcare provider.

  • Downtime: Redness for 1-2 hours
